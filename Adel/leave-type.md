# Leave Types (GGM Policy)

Reference table for all leave types configured in AbcHRM.

| Name | Slug | Allowance | Is Paid | Single Day | Track Balance | Visible to Staff | Gender | Description |
|------|------|-----------|---------|------------|---------------|------------------|--------|-------------|
| **Annual Leave** | annual | 30 days* | Yes | No | Yes | Yes | All | 21-30 days/year based on basic salary (not total salary) |
| **Casual Leave** | casual | 3/month | Yes | No | Yes | Yes | All | 3 Days per Month |
| **Sick Leave** | sick | - | Yes | No | Yes | No (HR only) | All | Provide Approved Sick Leave |
| **Unpaid Leave** | unpaid | - | No | No | No | Yes | All | Deduct from Salary (GGM) |
| **Tardy** | tardy | 3/month | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | All | 6-15 minutes, max 3 times per month |
| **Early Leave** | early_leave | 2/month | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | All | 1-2 hours same day, max 2 times per month |
| **Maternity Leave** | maternity | 365 days | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Female | 1 hour early before checkout daily for 1 year (e.g. 4:30 PM if normal 5:30 PM) |

---

## Notes

- **Annual Leave \***: 21 days/year for staff with &lt;5 years of service; 30 days/year for staff with ≥5 years of service.
- **Sick Leave**: Requires approved sick leave document; balance hidden from staff (visible to HR/GM only).
- **Unpaid Leave**: No balance tracking; days deducted from salary.
- **Tardy, Early Leave, Maternity**: Single-day only — start and end date must be the same.
